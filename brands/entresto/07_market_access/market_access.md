## 3. Prior Authorization Requirements

### 3.1 PA Landscape Summary

- **Overall PA requirement rate:** ~72% of plans require PA for Entresto
- **Average PA turnaround time (2023):** 4.2 days
- **First-round approval rate:** 68%
- **First-round denial rate:** 22%
- **Appeal success rate (Entresto Together hub):** 78%
- **Average time from denial to appeal resolution:** 8.3 days

### 3.2 Common PA Criteria Required by Payers

Most plans require documentation of one or more of the following before approving Entresto:
1. Confirmed HFrEF diagnosis (LVEF ≤40%) with echocardiogram or relevant imaging
2. NYHA Class II–IV documentation
3. ACE-I or ARB trial and failure or intolerance (step therapy requirement; see Section 4)
4. Prescriber is cardiologist, internist, or qualified NP/PA (some plans restrict to specialists)
5. Baseline labs: Creatinine, eGFR, serum potassium within 90 days
6. Documentation of concomitant beta-blocker therapy (GDMT requirement for some plans)

### 3.3 PA Documentation Package (Recommended)

| Document | Why Needed |
|---------|-----------|
| Most recent echocardiogram report (LVEF value) | Establishes HFrEF diagnosis |
| NYHA classification note from clinic visit | Confirms indication |
| Prior ACE-I/ARB prescription history (if applicable) | Step therapy documentation |
| Physician attestation of ACE-I intolerance (if switching) | Overcomes step therapy |
| NT-proBNP or BNP lab result (if using broadened label) | Supports LVEF 40–60% patients |
| 2022 ACC/AHA/HFSA guideline reference letter | Medical necessity for de novo ARNI |

## 4. Step Therapy Requirements

### 4.1 Overview

Approximately 58% of commercial plans require a documented trial of an ACE inhibitor or ARB before approving Entresto. This contradicts the 2022 ACC/AHA/HFSA guidelines, which give Entresto a Class I recommendation as the preferred first-line neurohormonal agent.

### 4.2 Step Therapy Challenge Strategy

**Medical necessity argument:**
> "The 2022 AHA/ACC/HFSA Heart Failure Guidelines state that sacubitril/valsartan is preferred over ACE inhibitor or ARB in patients with HFrEF (Class I, Level B-R). Requiring a trial and failure of an inferior therapy before prescribing the guideline-recommended preferred therapy is inconsistent with the published standard of care and may expose the patient to preventable risk of cardiovascular death or hospitalization. This patient has HFrEF with LVEF [X]% and clinical evidence of disease. We request a step therapy exception."

**State mandates:** 27 states have passed "step therapy reform" laws requiring exceptions when therapy contradicts clinical guidelines. Document state-specific mandates for territory reps.

**Step therapy override rate (Entresto Together hub):** 71% of step therapy exceptions approved when proper documentation is submitted.

### 4.3 Step Therapy Override Process

1. Rep identifies step therapy requirement during PA process
2. Hub services team contacts physician office for additional documentation
3. Hub generates guideline-based override letter with 2022 ACC/AHA reference
4. Submitted to payer's P&T override pathway
5. Average resolution time: 6.8 days
6. If denied: escalate to external IRO (Independent Review Organization) as available
