## 8. Strategic Recommendations for Q1–Q2 2026

1. **Accelerate FLOW data deployment** — CKD label expected Q2 2026; pre-position nephrologists now with MSL medical discussions; rep team ready to pivot to on-label renal messaging at label update.

2. **ESI formulary advantage pull-through** — 90 million lives with Ozempic preferred over Mounjaro at ESI. Deploy targeted access messaging to Tier 1/2 ESI-heavy practices; track pull-through TRx.

3. **Address persistence gap** — 52% 12-month persistence is below target trajectory. Recommend OzempicCare nursing touch at 3 months and 6 months (not just initiation). A/B test reminder app intervention.

4. **Post-call documentation compliance** — Mandatory Veeva SFE training Q1 2026; 90% target must be met for compliance and customer intelligence purposes.

5. **Wegovy cardiology activation** — SELECT CV indication is the unique hook; cardiology initiating Wegovy is a new prescriber behavior shift. Sustain momentum in CV segment.
