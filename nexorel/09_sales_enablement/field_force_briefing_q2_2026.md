### 2. Q2 Campaign: "The 35% Conversation"

**Core Concept:** 35% of EGFR+ NSCLC patients present with CNS metastases. Are your reps having the CNS conversation with every oncologist — not just the ones who've already had CNS failures?

**Campaign Tactics:**
- Leave-behind tool: "CNS Metastases Prevalence Card" — laminated quick reference for oncologist desks
- Digital ad campaign on Doceree and Veeva Engage targeting Tier 2 oncologists with CNS prevalence data
- Regional speaker programs: 12 events in Q2 featuring CNS-focused case studies (see Speaker Bureau calendar)

**Q2 Call Objective:**
At every sales call this quarter, reps should ask: *"Of your current EGFR+ patients, which ones came in with CNS disease or have since developed brain mets? Are you considering Nexorel for those patients specifically?"*

### 3. Payer Pull-Through: Step Edit Removal Campaign

**Background:** 21% of commercial payers still require osimertinib failure before approving Nexorel. This affects approximately 1,400 target HCPs in 14 states.

**Affected States:** TX, FL, OH, PA, MI, MO, TN, NC, SC, GA, IN, WI, MN, AZ

**What You Need to Do:**
1. Identify which patients in your territory are being denied Nexorel due to step edits (track via CoverMyMeds or Nexorel Navigator)
2. Provide affected oncologists with the "Payer Resource Kit" — includes prior auth support letter templates, NEXUS-1 CNS data summary formatted for P&T committees
3. Escalate persistent denials to your Regional Market Access Manager within 48 hours
4. Do NOT tell HCPs to prescribe Tagrisso first "to satisfy the step edit" — this is not appropriate clinical guidance and creates a compliance risk

**Goal:** Reduce step edit denial rate by 30% in affected territories by end of Q2.

### 4. Navigator Program Enrollment Drive

**Current Status:** 61% of new Nexorel patients are enrolled in Navigator. Target: 85% by Q3 2026.

**What's New in Q2:**
- Caregiver module launched March 15 — caregivers now receive their own welcome call and support materials
- 90-day co-pay bridge extended from 30 to 90 days for patients with pending PA
- New mobile app: patients can track their treatment schedule and side effects directly; integrates with Navigator call center

**Your Role:**
- At every new Nexorel prescription, confirm Navigator enrollment has been offered. Use the enrollment card; one page, one phone number.
- For hesitant patients: "There's no cost to enroll, and you'll have a nurse you can call anytime. Most patients say it's one of the most helpful parts of their treatment."
