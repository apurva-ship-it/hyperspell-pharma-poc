## 7. FEEDBACK LOOPS AND CONTINUOUS IMPROVEMENT

### 7.1 Monthly Analytics Review Cadence
| Meeting | Frequency | Participants | Output |
|---|---|---|---|
| US Brand Analytics Review | Monthly | US Brand Lead, Analytics, Market Access, SFE | KPI dashboard review; action items |
| Global Brand Performance Call | Monthly | Global Brand Lead + Regional heads | Global NRx/revenue tracking |

| Brand Tracker Debrief | Quarterly | Brand, Marketing Research | HCP/patient perception trends |
| Competitive Intelligence Update | Bi-monthly | Competitive Intel, Brand, MSLs | Competitive threat response |

### 7.2 Closed-Loop Feedback Process
1. **Signal collection**: CRM call notes, brand tracker, NPS detractor comments, PA denial data, digital engagement
2. **Analysis**: Analytics team weekly synthesis; identification of emerging patterns
3. **Hypothesis**: Marketing/medical hypothesize root cause
4. **Response**: Message adjustment, new materials, training intervention, MyWay program modification
5. **Measurement**: KPI delta at next review cycle (30, 60, 90-day checkpoints)
6. **Learning archive**: Lessons logged in Brand Intelligence System (BIS) for institutional memory
